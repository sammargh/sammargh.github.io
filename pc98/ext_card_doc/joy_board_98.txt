
JOY BOARD-98 (SNE)

Atari specification C bus joystick I / F. Not compatible with Windows 95.On board
There are 3 jumper switches.

・ JP1: I / O port address setting
　1 Short ■ 0188h to 018Ah (factory setting)
　2 short ■ 0288h ~ 028Ah

・ JP2: I / O port address setting
　1 short ■ E7E0h to E7E7h (factory setting)
　2 shorts ■ E5E0h to E5E7h
　3 short ■ E3E0h ~ E3E7h

・ JP3: Setting of machine model
　1 short ■ model with built-in FM sound source
　2 short ■ model without built-in FM sound source
