SCSI I / F in the PC-9801FA / FS / FX, MATE-A SCSI dedicated slot
There are two 8-dip dip switches and one 3-dip dip switch on the board.

There is no manual, so the setting contents of each switch are unknown.Of the ones at hand
The status of each switch is as follows.

・ 8 stations dip switch DSW1 = ON-ON-ON-OFF-OFF-OFF-OFF-ON-ON
・ 8 stations dip switch DSW2 = OFF-ON-ON-OFF-ON-ON-ON-ON
· Triple dip switch DSW3 = ON-ON-OFF
